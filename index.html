Legacy Insurance & Financial Services

Legacy is a reputable agency of experienced insurance professionals specializing in serving individuals who are in or approaching retirement. With a firm ethical commitment to our clients, we focus on providing tailored recommendations aimed at enhancing their financial security. Our expertise is centered around offering strategic improvements in five key areas of retirement planning.


1. Medicare

It can be complex, which is why we dedicate significant effort to educating our clients. We ensure they fully understand the key elements necessary to make informed decisions about their healthcare options.

2. Financial Security

We assist our clients in safeguarding their financial assets by enrolling them in programs designed to generate gains, eliminate the risk of losses, and ensure a lifetime of guaranteed income. Our focus is on providing safety, security, and dependable guarantees for your financial future!

3. Accelerated Debt Elimination

We are not a consumer credit counseling service, nor do we refinance debt. Instead, we help clients fully eliminate their debt using specialized insurance products. Our approach shows clients how to pay off all their debt— including their mortgage— in less than 9 years on average, without increasing their current spending.

4. Long-Term Care

Using insurance to cover some or all of the costs of long-term care not only ensures continued freedom of choice in your healthcare decisions but also provides an added layer of protection for your assets. This approach helps safeguard your financial security while giving you peace of mind, knowing that your future care needs are covered.

5. Life Insurance

Whether your goal is to leave a lasting legacy or simply cover final expenses, we offer a variety of outstanding options from highly-rated, trusted insurance companies with a long history of reliability.

We take great pride in our extensive expertise, as this is our full-time focus. Partnering with over 150 of the nation’s top-rated insurance companies, we are committed to providing the best solutions for our clients. We never recommend changes or additional products unless there is a clear benefit to improving your financial security. Our mission is to serve our clients through education, enhanced safety, and reliable protection, and we’re always here to assist you at any level you choose.
